## The Brief

A multi-location pizza and burger chain was paying over $15,000/month in delivery app commissions. They needed their own branded ordering experience that could handle high volume and encourage repeat business.

## What We Built

- High-speed ordering flow optimized for mobile (under 30 seconds to checkout)
- Combo meal builder with upsell suggestions
- Loyalty rewards system with points tracking
- Facebook Ads campaign targeting local foodie communities
- Integration with kitchen display systems

## Results

- Saved $12,000/month in platform commissions
- 300+ weekly orders through the direct system
- 35% repeat customer rate (vs. 8% on delivery platforms)
