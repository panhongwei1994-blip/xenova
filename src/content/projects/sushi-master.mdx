## The Brief

A high-end sushi restaurant wanted to break away from UberEats and build their own digital presence. They needed a website that conveyed premium quality, showcased their omakase menu, and allowed direct online ordering at 0% commission.

## What We Built

- Elegant, mobile-first showcase for seasonal menus and chef's specials
- Integrated ordering system with real-time order tracking
- Reservation module with capacity management
- Google Ads campaign driving local sushi enthusiasts to the site
- Multi-language support for English and Japanese

## Results

- 45% reduction in third-party platform dependency within 2 months
- 200+ direct orders per week through the independent system
- Google Ads campaign achieving 8:1 ROI
