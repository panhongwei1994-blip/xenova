## The Brief

A family-run Chinese restaurant wanted to reach both the local community and Chinese-speaking diaspora. They needed a bilingual digital presence that felt authentic and offered convenient ordering without relying on third-party apps.

## What We Built

- Bilingual website (English/Chinese) with automatic language detection
- Photo-rich menu with categories matching traditional Chinese dining style
- Direct ordering system with WeChat Pay integration
- Community marketing campaigns on WeChat and local Facebook groups
- Google Business profile optimization for local search

## Results

- 60% of online orders now come through the direct system
- Reached 2,000+ diaspora customers through targeted WeChat campaigns
- Google Maps ranking improved from page 3 to top 3 results
